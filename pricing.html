Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the one-time payment?

The one-time payment covers the complete design, development, and launch of your website. This includes all features listed in your chosen package, revisions, and initial setup.

Do I need a maintenance plan?

Yes, a maintenance plan is required to cover ongoing backend costs including hosting, security updates, and server management. These plans ensure your website remains secure, fast, and accessible 24/7. Choose the plan that best fits your budget and business needs.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your website at any time. We'll credit your original payment and only charge the difference plus any additional development time needed.

What if I need more pages than what's included?

Additional pages can be added for $25 per page. For larger projects, I can create a custom quote that better fits your needs.

Do you provide hosting?

I use 3rd party hosting included in the package, domain registration, and ongoing support to ensure your website runs smoothly.

What's your refund policy?

I am able to provide an 80% refund within the first 14 days of the websites release, However I do not charge until you look over the website and approve the design.
